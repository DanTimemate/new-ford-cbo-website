Our Organization

Our Vision

To create a self-sustaining Maasai community where every individual has access to education, economic opportunities, and cultural preservation, leading to improved quality of life and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower the Maasai community of Loita through holistic interventions in education, women's empowerment, infrastructure development, and economic sustainability while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

About New Ford CBO

Who We Are

New Ford CBO is a grassroots community-based organization founded and led by members of the Maasai community in Loita, Narok South. Registered and operating under Kenyan law, we serve as the last line of defense for our community after the departure of larger NGOs from our region.
